Transportation



For flying to Menlo Park you may use any of the SFO (18 miles), SJC (17 miles), or OAK (26 miles) airports.



Taxi fare is relatively expensive in California, so car rental or public transportation are more affordable. Public transportation options include Caltrain and BART.
