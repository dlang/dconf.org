DConf 2020 Canceled

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the D Language Foundation and our DConf 2020 sponsors, Symmetry Investments, came to the painful but correct decision to cancel DConf 2020. We hope to bring DConf back as soon as possible, be that in 2021 or some later date.

In the meantime, we've started a new tradition. DConf Online 2020 took place November 21 & 22, 2020. Details can be found on the DConf Online 2020 home page.
